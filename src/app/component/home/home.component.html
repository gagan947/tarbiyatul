Why Enroll with Us?

1. Live Classes
Live classes are offered to students in grades Pre-K to 4 four days a week.Our students experience a real classroom setting collaborated with their teacher and students.

2. Self Paced
Self-paced learning is offered for grades 1-4. Students can work at their own pace with the guidance of the Learning Coach. Self-paced students do not attend live classes and are guided by their Learning Coach. Teachers offer support when requested for further assistance with the course material. Self-paced students can enroll in religious studies courses for free. Self-paced students are welcome to school-wide events, and programs.

3. Academics
Our students have access to their digital curriculum accounts, from where they have access to their courses. This account is what the student needs to complete his coursework and assessments. Teachers make the utmost effort to ensure that-concepts that go against Islamic beliefs are not taught in our lessons. Our curriculum is a pre-designed digital platform, therefore parents should be aware that the course-ware includes concepts that go against Islamic beliefs. Teachers will point out before signing homework when the student should take caution and at times skip a particular section found in the lesson.

4. Annual Testing
We administer assessments to measure our student's learning. We use diagnostic assessments to measure our student's learning against their peers.

About Us

Committed Fun Filled Education

Our broad and flexible curriculum employs the best educational practices to ensure student success. A commitment to and belief in learning is important to all individuals and programs. We found that creative lesson planning plays a vital role in keeping students engaged and motivated throughout their educational journey. By incorporating innovative and interactive teaching techniques, you can capture your students' interest and make learning enjoyable. Our teachers are professional, dedicated, and innovative. They care about the children, and the children care about them. Our instructors are degree holders; however, this is not the only qualification that makes our teachers who they are. It is their dedication to the Islamic lifestyle that makes them much more: role models.
